How to play:

  • To begin, name your pharaoh and select the difficulty.
  • Every turn an event will happen that might help or hinder you.
  • During your turn, you can choose to fight an enemy army, make your army larger, consult an oracle, or build a wonder.
  • You will lose the game if you go bankrupt, lose all your soldiers, or lose your authority.
  • To win, complete 20 turns for easy, 35 turns for medium, or 50 turns for hard.
  • If you have enough glory by the end, you will get the good ending, otherwise you will get the bad ending.
